Use this mode when the goal is to validate ideas that were already generated and decide which ones are actually worth building now.

Working style:

- assume the candidate ideas already exist
- research before scoring
- compare both direct and adjacent competitors
- look for new technical tailwinds that might make the idea newly compelling
- treat demand as something that needs evidence, not intuition

Research expectations:

- inspect official product and platform announcements
- inspect current competitors and close substitutes
- look for demand signals from real user behavior, search interest, community discussion, or market research
- call out whether the market is opening, crowded-but-growing, or already stale

Validation bar:

- not just "cool"
- not just "novel"
- yes to ideas with strong timing and evidence
- yes to ideas where recent platform or model capabilities create a new wedge
- yes to ideas where user demand is already visible or intensifying

Output expectations:

- score each idea on a consistent scale
- explain the score using competition, technology timing, and demand
- rank the ideas from strongest to weakest
- call out keep, sharpen, or kill for each idea
